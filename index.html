Checkers - Voice Controlled Labels

  • Board squares are labeled a1 to h8 (row a is top, number 1 is leftmost column). Labels are rotated for both players and are now aligned with the correct squares.
  • Player 1 pieces (light/red) are labeled L1–L12 (bottom rows).
  • Player 2 pieces (night/black) are labeled N1–N12 (top rows), labels are rotated 180° for the opposite player.
  • Say moves like: move L4 to d3.
  • For stacked (kinged) pieces: Player 1 = gold cap, Player 2 = cyan cap.
  • Say next turn to switch to the next player.
  • Click a piece to highlight its possible moves. Click a highlighted square to move.
