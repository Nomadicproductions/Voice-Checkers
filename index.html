Checkers - Voice Controlled Labels

  • Board squares are labeled a3 to h10 (row a is top, number 3 is leftmost column). Squares or pieces with the number 2 are skipped to aid voice recognition. Row 3 is labeled as row 4, row 8 as row 9, etc., so there is no row labeled 2.
  • Player 1 pieces (light/red) are labeled L1, L3, L4, ... (skipping any label with a "2" in it, up to 12 labels).
  • Player 2 pieces (night/black) are labeled N1, N3, N4, ... (skipping "2", up to 12 labels), rotated 180° so the player sitting opposite can easily read them.
  • All board placement labels are spun 90° for player 2’s ease.
  • Say moves like: "move L3 to e4" (number 2 never appears in any label).
  • For stacked (kinged) pieces: Player 1 = gold cap, Player 2 = cyan cap.
  • Say "next turn" to switch to the next player.
  • Click a piece to highlight its possible moves. Click a highlighted square to move.
